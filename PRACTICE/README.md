# Practice & Assessment

Testing your knowledge before taking the actual AZ-104 exam is crucial for success. This section provides links to official practice assessments and community-recommended resources.

## 📝 Official Microsoft Practice Resources

Microsoft provides free resources to help you gauge your readiness for the exam.

### 1. Free Practice Assessment
* **Link**: [Take a free Practice Assessment](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/exams/az-104/practice/assessment?assessment-type=practice)
* **Overview**: This official practice assessment tests your skills with questions similar to those you will encounter on the actual exam. It provides immediate feedback and explanations for correct and incorrect answers.

### 2. Exam Sandbox
* **Link**: [Explore the Exam Sandbox](https://aka.ms/examdemo)
* **Overview**: Familiarize yourself with the exam user interface before test day. The sandbox allows you to experience the look and feel of the exam environment, including how to navigate between questions, mark items for review, and submit your exam.

## 💡 Exam Tips

1. **Understand Performance-Based Tasks**: The AZ-104 exam includes hands-on lab tasks. Practice using the Azure portal extensively.
2. **Focus on Networking**: Networking is heavily weighted in the exam. Ensure you understand VNets, NSGs, and load balancing thoroughly.
3. **Read Carefully**: Pay close attention to keywords like "most cost-effective," "highest availability," or "least administrative effort."
4. **Process of Elimination**: If you are unsure of an answer, eliminate the obviously incorrect options first.
5. **Understand the "Why"**: Don't just memorize answers; understand why an answer is correct and why the others are wrong.
6. **Review the Study Guide**: Regularly review the [official study guide](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/resources/study-guides/az-104) to ensure you are comfortable with all "Skills Measured."
